# PRODUCT.md

## What this is

**Abhi & Sanket** — WhatsApp-native sourcing agents for [Fleek](https://joinfleek.com), the B2B
vintage wholesale marketplace. Buyers text **Abhi** (buyer agent) on WhatsApp; **Sanket**
(supplier agent) negotiates behind the scenes within the buyer's mandate. Two agents, one thread.

## Who it's for

Resellers, vintage shops, and multi-channel retailers who source bulk secondhand fashion
(graded bales, small MOQs) and already live on WhatsApp.

## The web surface's single job

Convert a visitor into a WhatsApp conversation with Abhi. The landing page is a funnel, not a
product UI. Primary CTA everywhere: **Message Abhi on WhatsApp** → `https://wa.me/447424845871`.

Secondary surface: the catalog (`/collections/*`) — browse graded lots and source any one
with Abhi on WhatsApp.

## Register

`brand` — marketing landing; design IS the product here. Clean, calm, minimalist
(Linear-clean, not Awwwards-experimental). Whitespace and typography carry the quality; one
WhatsApp-green accent carries the action.
